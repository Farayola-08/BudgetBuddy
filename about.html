About BudgetBuddy

A friendly finance companion for students — learn, track, and grow your savings one step at a time.

Who We Are

BudgetBuddy is a student-focused financial wellness platform designed to help young people take control of their money, build healthy spending habits, and grow with confidence. We simplify budgeting and financial learning so students can focus on what matters most.

Our Mission

To empower students with accessible tools and easy-to-understand guidance that build lifelong financial habits and reduce money-related stress.

Our Vision

To make financial literacy a common skill among young people, and to expand support to communities and small vendors in the future.

What We Do

Expense Tracking

Easily record daily spending and review where your money goes.

Savings Goals

Set targets, track progress, and celebrate milestones.

Financial Tips

Short, student-friendly advice to build smarter money habits.

Impact Goals

We aim to improve financial literacy, reduce unnecessary spending, encourage savings, and support students to make confident financial decisions.
